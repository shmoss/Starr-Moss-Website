Welcome to Madison!

Madison is the crown jewel as well as the capital of Wisconsin. Flanked by two serene lakes, Monona and Mendota, it blends big city culture with small town charm. Madison is the definition Midwestern hospitality and is consistently ranked as one of the best places to live in the US. There are many reasons why Madison is such an amazing place to live, from the more than 200 miles of scenic biking and hiking trails in the area, locally grown food served by many restaurants, and a variety of festivals and nightlife to keep you busy throughout the year.
