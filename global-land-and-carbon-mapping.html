Global land and carbon mapping

We work to improve mapping of land and its biomass and soil carbon worldwide

Understanding the distribution of land and terrestrial carbon across space and timeis vital to helping navigate climate change and identify solutionsto preserve carbon stocks, protect biodiversity, and reduce greenhouse gas emissions from the agricultural, forestry, and other land use sectors. GLUE researchers have developed some of the world’s leading datasets on global degraded land locations, above-and belowground biomass carbon stocks, and other land and carbon products. Our team, collaborators, and scientists around the world use these data help inform analyses of sustainable agricultural development, conservation, and Natural Climate Solutions, among other applications.
