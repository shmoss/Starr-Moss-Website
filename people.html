Meet Our Team

Our researchers come from diverse fields to discover new ways to under how and why people use land around the world. GLUE is home to many accomplished individuals and together we form a strong community committed to values of diversity, equity and inclusion. We aim to reconcile forest conservation, climate change, and food security by informing policy and market-based strategies.
